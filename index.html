Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our data.

How do you ensure data accuracy?
We run a live 5-step verification process the moment you initiate an export. This includes syntax checks, domain validation, and real-time SMTP pings to ensure the email is actively accepting mail.
Are you GDPR and CCPA compliant?
Yes, entirely. We only scrape publicly available B2B information and we maintain a strict opt-out registry. We provide full compliance documentation for enterprise clients upon request.
Can I integrate this with HubSpot or Salesforce?
Absolutely. We offer native, one-click integrations for HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and Outreach. You can also use our Zapier app or open API to connect to thousands of other tools.
What happens if an email bounces?
We guarantee a 95% deliverability rate. If you ever experience a hard bounce rate higher than 5% on our verified data, we will automatically refund the credits used for those specific leads.
Can I export data to Excel or CSV?
Yes, absolutely. You can easily export any generated list directly to a highly formatted CSV or Excel file. This is standard on all tiers and allows for seamless uploads to platforms we don't natively integrate with.
Do you offer a free trial or sample data?
Yes! We provide 50 free verification credits when you sign up—no credit card required. You can run real queries, verify contacts, and see the exact quality of our engine before committing to a paid plan.
How often is the underlying database updated?
Our proprietary engine runs continuously. While we perform live-verifications at the moment of export, the baseline database itself undergoes a complete rolling refresh every 30 days to catch job changes and company acquisitions early.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
There are no long-term lock-ins. All of our standard plans are billed month-to-month. You can cancel, pause, or upgrade your subscription at any time directly from your billing dashboard.
